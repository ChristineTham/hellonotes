# HelloNotes

A native Markdown knowledge base for your notes.

Press → to advance.

---

## Why HelloNotes

- Your notes are **plain Markdown files** on disk
- Backlinks, tags, and full-text search
- Git versioning built in
- A multi-provider AI assistant

---

## Slides, too

This very note is a **Marp** deck:

1. Add `marp: true` to the front matter
2. Separate slides with `---`
3. Click the slides button in the editor bar
